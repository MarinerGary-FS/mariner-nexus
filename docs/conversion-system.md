# Mariner Nexus — Conversion System

## Core Philosophy

Conversion is not a button.

It is the result of:
- perception
- clarity
- trust
- positioning
- timing

---

## User Journey

### Stage 1 — Awareness
User lands on site

Goal:
“This feels different.”

---

### Stage 2 — Alignment
User explores content

Goal:
“This applies to me.”

---

### Stage 3 — Proof
User views case studies

Goal:
“They’ve done this before.”

---

### Stage 4 — Value
User understands services

Goal:
“This would help my business.”

---

### Stage 5 — Action
User takes next step

Goal:
“I want to work with them.”

---

## Primary Conversion

### Book Strategy Call
- embedded calendar (Cal.com)
- low friction
- high clarity

---

## Secondary Conversion

### Contact Form
- routed to: info@ or sales@
- used for:
  - custom inquiries
  - partnerships

---

## Email Routing

### info@
- general intake
- automated responses

---

### sales@
- qualified leads
- follow-up
- proposals

---

### gary@
- high-level conversations
- partnerships
- closing layer

---

## CTA Strategy

### Placement
- hero section
- after case studies
- after services
- final page section

---

### Language
- direct
- confident
- minimal

Examples:
- Book Strategy Call
- Start Your Build

---

## Friction Reduction

- no long forms
- clear next step
- fast loading pages
- mobile-friendly flow

---

## Trust Signals

- case studies
- structured explanation
- premium design
- clear thinking

---

## Key Principle

Do not push.

Guide.

---

## Objective

The user should move naturally toward action without feeling pressured.

Final outcome:

“I’m ready to talk to them.”
