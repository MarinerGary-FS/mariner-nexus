# Mariner Nexus Skills Reference

## Purpose
This document defines the cognitive and creative skill models used across Mariner Nexus projects.
These skills should be applied selectively depending on the type of build.

---

## Core Skills

### 1. Cinematic Storytelling
Use scene-based progression, visual rhythm, and intentional pacing.
Best for:
- homepage experiences
- case studies
- premium landing pages

### 2. First-Principles Thinking
Reduce every decision to purpose, mechanism, and outcome.
Best for:
- messaging
- UX decisions
- offer design
- page architecture

### 3. Conversion Engineering
Design each section to move the user toward belief and action.
Best for:
- landing pages
- service pages
- lead generation
- CTA architecture

### 4. Premium UI/UX Design Thinking
Use disciplined spacing, strong hierarchy, and minimal clutter.
Best for:
- all frontend builds
- visual systems
- interactive presentations

### 5. Systems Thinking
Treat each build as an interconnected business ecosystem.
Best for:
- CRM-connected sites
- automation-driven builds
- service architecture
- operational pages

### 6. Brand Positioning
Control how the company is perceived through structure, tone, and presentation.
Best for:
- homepage messaging
- about pages
- case studies
- proposals

### 7. Attention Engineering
Direct the user’s focus intentionally.
Best for:
- hero sections
- section sequencing
- motion design
- conversion pages

### 8. Perception Design
Increase perceived value through restraint, polish, and precision.
Best for:
- premium brands
- luxury-style experiences
- executive-facing pages

### 9. Performance Awareness
Balance motion and experience quality with fast loading and device compatibility.
Best for:
- animation-heavy builds
- homepage experiences
- mobile optimization

### 10. Modular Build Thinking
Create reusable structures, systems, and component logic.
Best for:
- multi-page sites
- repeated case study frameworks
- scalable design systems

---

## Usage Guidance

### Use on every project by default
- First-Principles Thinking
- Premium UI/UX Design Thinking
- Systems Thinking
- Brand Positioning
- Performance Awareness
- Modular Build Thinking

### Use when building premium brand experiences
- Cinematic Storytelling
- Attention Engineering
- Perception Design

### Use when building revenue-focused pages
- Conversion Engineering
- Attention Engineering
- Brand Positioning

### Use when building case studies
- Cinematic Storytelling
- First-Principles Thinking
- Conversion Engineering
- Systems Thinking
- Perception Design
